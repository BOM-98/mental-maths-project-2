Welcome To The Arithmetic Coach!

The Mental Maths Coach offers a dynamic environment for practicing addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division, with problems that automatically adjust in difficulty to keep you challenged and engaged. The game will keep moving up in levels, showing you increasingly more difficult sums, until you are getting them correct roughly 85% of the time - which has shown to be the optimal ratio for learning a new skill. To move up a level, you need to have answered 4 questions on that level, in under 60 seconds each, while maintaining an 85% score.


Your score is calculated and displayed in a green box at the top of the screen. It will automatically adjust as you answer each sum. You will need to reach a score higher than 85% to be eligible to progress to the next level. You can only move to the next level once you have answered 4 questions on your current level.


There are 18 levels in total - only mathematical geniuses will make it to level 18. The game does not end, so you can keep playing as long as you like.
Good luck!
